About Ithihad Friends

Ithihad Friends is the vibrant class union of Darunnajath Islamic Complex, Vallapuzha, dedicated to nurturing unity, leadership, and academic excellence among students. By organizing engaging events, collaborative projects, and faith-based activities, it strengthens bonds of friendship and fosters a spirit of teamwork. Rooted in Islamic values, the union encourages creativity, mutual respect, and personal growth. Serving as a platform for dialogue and shared learning, Ithihad Friends helps students develop both intellectually and spiritually, creating a positive and inspiring classroom environment.
